1. Introduction

YourSpace ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our social networking platform.

By using YourSpace, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, username, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Display name, bio, location, profile picture, custom HTML/CSS
  • Content: Posts, comments, messages, wall comments, photos, videos
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store card details)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, features used, interactions
  • Cookies: Authentication tokens, session data, preferences

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Firebase/Google: Authentication and hosting services
  • Stripe: Payment processing and payout information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Platform Operation: Provide, maintain, and improve YourSpace services
  • Account Management: Create and manage your account, authenticate users
  • Communication: Enable messaging, notifications, and user interactions
  • Payments: Process virtual reward purchases and creator payouts
  • Safety & Security: Detect fraud, prevent abuse, enforce Terms & Conditions
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns, improve features, optimize performance
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations, respond to requests

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 Public Information

The following information is publicly visible to all users:

  • Username and display name
  • Profile picture and bio
  • Public posts and comments
  • Profile customizations (themes, backgrounds)

4.2 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers:

  • Firebase/Google Cloud: Hosting, database, authentication
  • Stripe: Payment processing, creator payouts
  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request.

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred.

4.5 With Your Consent

We may share information with your explicit consent for specific purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Data Storage

  • Location: Data stored on Google Firebase servers (U.S. and global locations)
  • Duration: Data retained while your account is active
  • Backup: Regular backups for data recovery

5.2 Security Measures

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Firebase security rules
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

5.3 Limitations

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Update

You can access and update your information through your profile settings.

6.2 Data Deletion

You can request account deletion by contacting us at contact@yourspace.com. Note:

  • Some data may be retained for legal/operational purposes
  • Public posts may remain visible (anonymized)
  • Transaction history retained for tax compliance

6.3 Opt-Out Options

  • Email notifications: Disable in account settings
  • Browser notifications: Disable in browser settings
  • Cookies: Clear browser cookies (may affect functionality)

6.4 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we do not sell personal information)

6.5 European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

EU residents have additional rights:

  • Right to access, rectify, erase personal data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authority

YourSpace is not intended for users under 13 years of age.
