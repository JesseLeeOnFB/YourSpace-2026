About YourSpace

YourSpace is a modern social media platform where users can create personalized profiles, share content, connect with friends, and support creators through virtual gifts.

Similar to platforms like TikTok and Twitch, we've built a creator economy where users can monetarily support their favorite content creators through digital gift purchases.

Virtual Gifts System

Our platform features a comprehensive virtual gifts marketplace with 18 different digital items. Users purchase these gifts to send to content creators as a form of appreciation and support.

How Virtual Gifts Work:
  1. User views content from another user/creator
  2. User clicks "Send Gift" button on posts
  3. User selects a virtual gift and completes payment via Stripe
  4. Gift appears on recipient's profile as a badge/counter
  5. Recipient receives a revenue share (6-40% depending on gift value)
  6. Creator earnings are paid out every 14 days via Stripe Connect

Business Model

YourSpace operates as a digital gift marketplace and social platform:

  • Revenue Model: Platform fee on virtual gift transactions
  • Platform Fee: 60-94% (industry standard for virtual goods/digital content platforms)
  • Creator Payout: 6-40% depending on gift value and transaction fees
  • Payout Schedule: Every 14 days via Stripe Connect
  • Product Type: Non-refundable digital virtual items
Transaction Flow:
User purchases virtual gift ($1.99-$300) ‚Üí Stripe processes payment ‚Üí Platform takes fee ‚Üí Creator receives revenue share ‚Üí Payout every 14 days to creator's bank account
